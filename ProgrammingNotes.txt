Zapiski programiranja za dokumentacijo:

PAGER ADAPTER = adapter to populate pages inside ViewPager, skrbi za content
	FRAGMENT PAGE ADAPTER = implementation of PAGER ADAPTER that represents each page as a FRAGMENT
	
VIEW PAGER = layout adapter (ViewGroup) -> swiping left and right through pages of data. This data
			 is generated by PAGER ADAPTER.
